Sydney, Australia

Sydney, Australia is the state capital of New South Wales and the most populous city in Australia and Oceania.

More About

The most significant sight of Sydney is Sydney Opera House, the true wonder of 20th century architecture. The "Petals" of the Opera House are located on the water. Both the exterior and the interior of the building are beautiful. It is worth visiting the Opera House and learning about its history. The observation deck is one of the city's most recent sights. It is located at the top of the Sydney Tower at an altitude of 260 m above Sydney. There is a rotating glass floor with platforms extending to the very edge. From here you can see the whole city by 360 degrees. . The city has many beautiful beaches, and most of them are considered to be the most important Bondi Beach. This is a great tourist attraction. In the summer, you can sunbathe here and surf. In the immediate vicinity of this beach are shops and restaurants. Darling Harbor is another attractive place, the ship's docks were located there earlier. Now they have become an exhibition center, popular, mostly among children. The Cockle Wharf restaurant and café complex make Darling Harbor more attractive.
